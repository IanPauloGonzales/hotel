Room Features

Regency

Features 1 Queen Size Bed, LED TV with cable/in-house movies, air conditioning, dining table, hot and cold bath, and bathtub.

Regency II

Bigger groups like families and barkadas will love the Regency Room II. It features 1 queen size bed, 1 single bed, LED TV with cable/in-house movies, air-conditioning, dining table, hot and cold bath, and bathtub.

Premium Room

The Premium Room is perfect for solo travelers and couples. Snug, cozy, with a twin size bed, air-conditioning, LED TV with cable channels and in-house movies, plus a hot and cold shower.

Employee Information
